About Us

Where Champions Are Made

C.O.R.E. Performance has been Georgia's most trusted indoor training facility since 2018. Founded by former professional players, our mission is simple: give every athlete the tools, instruction, and environment to reach their full potential.

Precision-Focused Instruction
Data-driven coaching using video analysis and launch angle technology to fine-tune mechanics.
All Ages & Skill Levels
Programs for tee-ballers through competitive high school and collegiate athletes.
Professional-Grade Equipment
Pitching machines, radar guns, turf infields, and HitTrax simulation systems.
